# Toronto & Ontario Vulnerable-Population Service Sources

A directory of authoritative sources for locating homeless shelters, warming/respite centres, community housing, and other city/provincial services for vulnerable populations. Useful for a directory app, safety/services map, or just finding help. Phone numbers and addresses change seasonally — always confirm against the live City pages.

## Emergency Access & Intake (start here)
* **[Toronto Central Intake](https://www.toronto.ca/community-people/housing-shelter/homeless-help/central-intake/)** — City-operated 24/7 phone line for emergency shelter referrals. Call **416-338-4766** / **1-877-338-3398**, or **311**.
* **[Streets to Homes Assessment & Referral Centre (SHARC)](https://211ontario.ca/service/94638117/)** — Walk-in shelter referral and housing help at 129 Peter St.
* **[211 Ontario](https://211ontario.ca/)** / **[211 Central](https://211central.ca/)** — Province-wide directory of social services and community supports. Dial **211**. Searchable by topic and location; backs much of the data below.
* **[City of Toronto Homeless Help hub](https://www.toronto.ca/community-people/housing-shelter/homeless-help/)** — Master landing page linking to all shelter, respite, and housing services.

## Shelters & Overnight Services
* **[Daily Shelter & Overnight Service Occupancy & Capacity (Open Data)](https://open.toronto.ca/dataset/daily-shelter-overnight-service-occupancy-capacity/)** — Daily program-level occupancy/vacancy/capacity (2021–present) from the SMIS system, with operator, location, and classification. **CKAN API available** for live querying.
* **[Daily Shelter Occupancy (historic, 2017–2020)](https://ckan0.cf.opendata.inter.prod-toronto.ca/en/dataset/daily-shelter-occupancy)** — Pre-COVID base-shelter dataset on the Open Data portal.
* **[Daily Shelter & Overnight Service Usage](https://www.toronto.ca/city-government/data-research-maps/research-reports/housing-and-homelessness-research-and-reports/shelter-census/)** — City research page explaining the 4 a.m. snapshot methodology.
* **[Shelter System Flow Data](https://www.toronto.ca/city-government/data-research-maps/research-reports/housing-and-homelessness-research-and-reports/shelter-system-flow-data/)** — Monthly counts of people entering/leaving the shelter system.

## Warming Centres & Winter Respite
* **[Warming Centres](https://www.toronto.ca/community-people/housing-shelter/homeless-help/toronto-warming-centres/)** — Locations + sign up for e-Updates on open/close status. Activated around -5 °C or a winter weather warning; walk-in, pet-friendly, no intake required.
* **[Winter Services Plan for People Experiencing Homelessness](https://www.toronto.ca/community-people/housing-shelter/homeless-help/about-torontos-shelter-system/winter-services-plan-for-people-experiencing-homelessness/)** — Annual plan listing seasonal respite sites and capacity.
* **[24-hour respite / drop-ins / warming centres / Out of the Cold (311 article)](https://www.toronto.ca/home/311-toronto-at-your-service/find-service-information/article/?kb=kA06g000001cvOlCAI)** — Consolidated 311 listing.
* **[Toronto Shelter Network — Winter Resources](http://www.torontoshelternetwork.com/winter-resources)** — Nonprofit-curated winter resource list.

## Community / Social / Subsidized Housing
* **[Rent-Geared-to-Income (RGI) Subsidy — City of Toronto](https://www.toronto.ca/community-people/employment-social-support/housing-support/rent-geared-to-income-subsidy/)** — How to apply to Toronto's Centralized Waiting List for subsidized housing.
* **[Toronto Community Housing — Subsidized / RGI](https://www.torontohousing.ca/rent/subsidized-housing)** — Largest social-housing provider in the city.
* **[Social Housing Waiting List Reports](https://www.toronto.ca/city-government/data-research-maps/research-reports/housing-and-homelessness-research-and-reports/social-housing-waiting-list-reports/)** — Published waitlist statistics.
* **[SocialHousingApplication.com](https://www.socialhousingapplication.com/)** — Centralized online application used by many Ontario service-manager areas.
* Regional service managers (each runs its own centralized waitlist): **[York Region](https://www.york.ca/support/housing/applying-subsidized-housing)**, **[Region of Waterloo](https://www.regionofwaterloo.ca/en/living-here/find-affordable-housing.aspx)**, **[Niagara Regional Housing](https://www.niagararegion.ca/housing-homelessness/nrh/find-a-unit/)**, **[Ottawa Housing Registry](https://housingregistry.ca/the-process/)**.

## Food, Drop-ins & Meals
* **[Food Banks — Toronto Central Healthline](https://www.torontocentralhealthline.ca/mapMultipleServices.aspx?id=10572)** — Mapped directory of food banks across the city.
* **[The Stop Community Food Centre](https://www.thestop.org/)** — Food bank, drop-in meals, community kitchens, and good-food market.
* Use **211 / 211 Central** (above) for the most complete, location-aware drop-in and meal listings.

## Harm Reduction & Health
* **[Harm Reduction Supplies & Locations — Toronto Public Health](https://www.toronto.ca/community-people/health-wellness-care/health-programs-advice/harm-reduction-supplies-and-locations/)** — 60+ agencies, 100+ access points across the city.
* **[Supervised Consumption Sites Dashboard — Health Infobase (Canada)](https://health-infobase.canada.ca/supervised-consumption-sites/)** — National dashboard of authorized sites.
* **[Supervised consumption sites explained — Health Canada](https://www.canada.ca/en/health-canada/services/substance-use/supervised-consumption-sites/explained.html)** — Types of sites and services.

## Open Data, Counts & Research
* **[City of Toronto Open Data Portal](https://open.toronto.ca/)** — CKAN-based portal; search "shelter", "housing", "homeless" for machine-readable datasets + API.
* **[Everyone Counts — Point-in-Time Counts (Housing, Infrastructure & Communities Canada)](https://housing-infrastructure.canada.ca/homelessness-sans-abri/resources-ressources/point-in-time-denombrement-ponctuel-eng.html)** — Nationally coordinated single-night enumeration + survey (annual as of 2025); Ontario grouped in the Central region.
* **[Homelessness Learning Hub — PiT Counts](https://homelessnesslearninghub.ca/point-in-time-counts/)** — Methodology and resources.
